# Personas and Roles

## Customer personas

### Customer Admin
Usually a marketing lead, RevOps lead, or manager.
Needs:
- visibility into projects
- ability to invite colleagues
- billing and usage clarity
- confidence that work is progressing

### Customer Member
Usually an operator or stakeholder.
Needs:
- ability to view relevant projects
- comment and provide feedback
- access deliverables and assets

### Customer Viewer
Usually an executive or finance stakeholder.
Needs:
- read-only visibility
- invoices and status visibility

## Internal personas

### Provider
A consultant, CSM, or account manager.
Needs:
- assigned customer and project visibility
- contextual communication
- asset upload and update workflows
- operational clarity

### Admin
Internal Superwork operator with platform-level access.
Needs:
- control over customers, memberships, and interventions
- auditability
- safe overrides

## System roles

### User types
- customer
- provider
- admin

### Organization membership roles
- admin
- member
- viewer
